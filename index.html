Your Pathway to ICT Confidence and Compliance

Addressing the urgent need for digitally competent teachers under Zambia's Enhanced Competency-Based Curriculum (ECBC)

The Challenge for Zambian Teachers

The integration of ICT as a compulsory subject in Zambian schools has created an urgent need for:

  • Digitally competent teachers across all subject areas
  • Practical exposure to emerging ICT fields
  • Continuous professional development outside traditional colleges
  • Affordable, structured, and supportive learning environments

Despite policy progress, many teachers face constraints such as:

  • Limited access to modern computer laboratories
  • Inadequate opportunities for advanced ICT training
  • Lack of guided self-learning environments
  • High personal costs for independent online learning

How FutureSkills Addresses These Gaps

FutureSkills ICT & Digital Skills Centre addresses these gaps by offering a district-level, teacher-centered ICT learning ecosystem that blends self-paced online study with structured local support.

1

Assess Your Skill Gap

Identify whether you are an ICT teacher needing advanced skills or a non-ICT teacher requiring foundational digital competence for ECBC implementation.

2

Access Curated Learning Paths

Follow specially designed tracks with courses from global platforms like Coursera, edX, and Cisco NetAcad, curated for the Zambian educational context.

3

Learn in Guided Environment

Study in our modern computer labs with facilitator assistance, peer collaboration, and practical assignments that translate theory to classroom practice.

4

Earn Global Certification

Complete courses and earn certificates from globally recognized platforms while building practical competence for your classroom.

Our Background & Rationale

FutureSkills ICT & Digital Skills Centre evolved from foundational computer training into a specialized teacher upskilling and digital competence hub, responding to:

  • National education reforms in Zambia
  • Rapid technological change impacting all sectors
  • The implementation of the Republic of Zambia Enhanced Competency-Based Curriculum (ECBC)
  • The urgent need for teacher capacity building in ICT

Our primary focus is to support teachers with the ICT knowledge, confidence, and practical exposure required to effectively deliver compulsory ICT education in schools across Zambia.

Compliance & Regulatory Positioning

FutureSkills operates strategically as a facilitated digital learning centre:

  • We provide modern infrastructure, guided study environments, and mentorship
  • Learning content is delivered via third-party global platforms (Coursera, edX, Cisco, etc.)
  • Certification is issued externally by course providers
  • We do not issue our own academic certificates or diplomas

This model ensures we operate without undue TEVET registration requirements while fully supporting national ICT skills objectives and maintaining regulatory clarity.
